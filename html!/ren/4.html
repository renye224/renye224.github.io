You enter the depths of the sewers.
LEFT

From the left of the sewer you see a faint orange glowing light. The muffled sounds of a terrible movie play in the background as someone idly plucks the strings of an electric guitar.

FORWARD

Right in front of you, you see a faint green glowing light. The path forward is somehow covered in moss and mushrooms of a forest floor. Ribbit...ribbit...ribbit...

RIGHT

From the right of the sewer you see a faint turquoise glowing light. Everything, from the stones of the tunnel walls to the scum of the sewer itself is somehow colored in brilliant shades of green and blue. You hear faint accented laughter in the distance.
